---
title: Less than Zero
tags: [ "business", "vibes", “the-market” ]
---

Layoffs, profits, and The Quarterly Cycle™  create headlines,
discussion, and narratives that make $1bn in quarterly profit
look like less than zero. Breaking even, a milestone for any
growing business, would be catastrophic.

Public companies that make $10bn in profit every quarter, but
don’t increase that profit, are perceived as effectively
$0-revenue businesses.
